Privacy Policy - Sunnahly

Last Updated: December 26, 2024

1. Introduction

The Sunnahly app ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Data Collection

The Sunnahly app collects the following data:

  • Location: We collect your approximate location (latitude and longitude) to calculate accurate prayer times for your city. Location data is only collected when you grant permission to access geolocation.
  • App Settings: Your app settings (selected language, prayer time calculation method, notification settings, selected prayers for notifications and azan) are stored locally on your device.

3. Data Usage

Location data is used exclusively for:

  • Calculating prayer times for your location
  • Providing accurate prayer times (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha)
  • Calculating sunrise time
  • Calculating the last third of the night (Tahajjud time)

Important: All data is stored locally on your device. We DO NOT transfer, sell, or share your data with third parties.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS/Android storage mechanisms. We do not use cloud services to store your data.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable location access in iOS/Android settings at any time
  • Use the app without location access (you can enter coordinates manually)
  • Delete the app at any time - all your data will be deleted along with the app
  • Configure which prayers to receive notifications for
  • Configure which prayers to play azan sound for

6. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Aladhan API: For obtaining prayer times. We only send coordinates (latitude and longitude) without any personal information. The API does not store your data.

7. Notifications

The app sends local notifications for prayer times. You can:

  • Enable or disable notifications in app settings
  • Choose which prayers to receive notifications for
  • Configure notification timing (how many minutes before prayer)
  • Enable or disable azan sound for notifications

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted over the internet, except for API requests to calculate prayer times (coordinates only).

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
